The Greatest Movies According to Kolu

Kolu's top 3 movies of all-time.

Bahubali 2: The Conclusion

Directed by S.S. Rajamouli, Bahubali 2: The Conclusion is a 2017 Indian epic action film that follows the story of Amarendra Baahubali, who learns about his heritage, and begins to look for answers. The film stars Prabhas, Rana Daggubati, Anushka Shetty, and Tamannaah in lead roles.

Avengers: Endgame

Directed by Anthony and Joe Russo, Avengers: Endgame is a 2019 American superhero film that follows the Avengers as they assemble once more in order to undo the damage caused by Thanos in the previous film. The film stars Robert Downey Jr., Chris Evans, Mark Ruffalo, Chris Hemsworth, Scarlett Johansson, and Jeremy Renner.
